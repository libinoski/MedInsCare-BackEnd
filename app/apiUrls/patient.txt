
1. [Patient Authentication and Profile Management]
   - Patient login route: `POST http://localhost:1313/api/patient/patientLogin`
   - Change patient password route: `POST http://localhost:1313/api/patient/patientChangePassword`
   - Change patient's ID proof image route: `POST http://localhost:1313/api/patient/patientChangeIdProofImage`
   - Change patient's profile image route: `POST http://localhost:1313/api/patient/patientChangeProfileImage`
   - View patient's profile route: `POST http://localhost:1313/api/patient/patientViewProfile`
   - View patient's associated hospital profile route: `POST http://localhost:1313/api/patient/patientViewHospitalProfile`
   - Update patient's profile route: `POST http://localhost:1313/api/patient/patientUpdateProfile`

2. [News and Information]
   - View all news available to the patient route: `POST http://localhost:1313/api/patient/patientViewAllNews`
   - View a specific piece of news route: `POST http://localhost:1313/api/patient/patientViewOneNews`

3. [Insurance Providers and Packages]
   - View all insurance providers available to the patient route: `POST http://localhost:1313/api/patient/patientViewAllInsuranceProviders`
   - View details of a specific insurance provider route: `POST http://localhost:1313/api/patient/patientViewOneInsuranceProvider`
   - View all insurance packages available to the patient route: `POST http://localhost:1313/api/mic/patient/patientViewAllInsurancePackages`
   - View details of a specific insurance package route: `POST http://localhost:1313/api/patient/patientViewOneInsurancePackage`
   - Choose an insurance package route: `POST http://localhost:1313/api/patient/patientChooseInsurancePackage`
   - Patient search insurance providers route: `POST http://localhost:1313/api/patient/patientSearchInsuranceProviders`
   - Patient review one insurance provider route: `POST http://localhost:1313/api/mic/patient/patientReviewOneInsuranceProvider`
